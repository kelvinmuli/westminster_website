Project Analysis

The firm provides a full range of professional services including Tax Strategy and Operations, Tax Compliance & Audit Services, Tax Objections & Dispute Resolutions, Trade and Tax Advisory, formulating Trade and Tax Policies, Tax Administration, Audit and Assurance services to leading multinational and local corporations as well as high net worth individuals across diverse industries.

Investment Analysis

Our firm’s strength lies in the ability to offer practical and personalized services and solutions to complex, and often intriguing problems faced by our clients. We value client confidentiality and are committed to ensuring that our clientele get value for money through provision of efficient, timely and reliable services. The needs of our clients come first.

Global Operation

The firm is led by highly qualified professionals with extensive experience engaging revenue authorities, regulators & government agencies across diverse jurisdictions. With deep knowledge of tax legislation and trade principles, the team develops customized solutions that address complex client needs. Over the years, the partners have advised clients in Kenya, Australia, Bermuda, Tanzania, the United Kingdom, and Uganda.
